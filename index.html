Meerut Institute of Engineering and Technology

The Meerut Institute of engineering and Technology was founded in 1997 and is managed by the Meerut, Uttar Pradesh Educational & Social Welfare Society.
MIET college is accredited by the All India Council for Technical Education, Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India, New Delhi, and is affiliated with Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam University .

Courses We Offer

The Meerut Institute of Engineering and Technology offers a variety of UG and PG courses, with admission granted based on merit in the qualifying examination and entrance exam scores. It accepts scores from various state and national-level entrance exams such as JEE Main, CAT, MAT, and others.

Our facilities

The Meerut Institute of Engineering and Technology has a hostel, library, and other facilities that allow students to have a stress-free learning experience throughout their college career. It also provides students with facilities such as sports, mess, and so on to give them a break from their regular academic schedule. The college's sports facilities assist them in staying physically and mentally fit.
